What The Fish Collection is a collection of unique Fish NTFs, which give you membership access to upcoming fishing contest. Each Fish is algorithmically generated with AI, named and mixed with an AI generated style, that is inspired by the hand-selected greatest works of the most famous painters (like Da Vinci, Van Gogh, Picasso, Monet, Klimt, Dali, Gaugin), but also by the works of culture (hieroglyph, firework, fractal) and nature (clouds, moss, rocks).

The collection is released in parts with limited quantities on the Ethereum blockchain.

Catch your fish from What The Fish Collection!

UPCOMING

Each Fish allows you to take part in a metapond fishing contest that will be helded once a day. During the contest your fish(es) is(are) staked into the virtual pond, and you take part in the fishing. Each contest ends with ranking of who catched the best fish. The more fish you put into the pond, the greater your chance of catching. The first competition is scheduled after the initial sale, most likely in June 2022.

After the first competition, the first minting will take place. What The Fish Collection holders will have one hour priority for each subsequent minting. During each release the top winners in the current rankings will be given a free Mint.
