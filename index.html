The carefully hand-selected collection of unique paintings, created with AI imagination, is inspired by the greatest works of art, culture, and nature. The paintings and AI generated fish images were provided as an input for the drawing AI. In What The Fish collection, even a fish can be portrayed by visionaries like Leonardo Da Vinci, Vincent Van Gogh, Pablo Picasso, Claude Monet, Wassily Kandinsky, Gustav Klimt, Salvatore Dali, Paul Gaugin, or present itself as a hieroglyph, firework, drawings or fractal.
